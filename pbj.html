PB&J

by Brandon


When I make this breakfast? I make this breakfast whenever I dont have time for a big one. Weekday Mornings.
Why I make this breakfast? I love peanut butter and the sweet salty taste combo with the jelly is unmatched.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

What to Do

  1. Toast your bread to your liking.
  2. Spread alot of jelly on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread more peanut butter on the other slice.
  4. Place the two slices together. Do not smush it.
  5. Enjoy
