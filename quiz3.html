Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

by Brandon

This is a great, quick and easy snack that can be eaten at anytime of the day. Its high in protein (peanut butter) and good for your sweet tooth (jelly). The sweet salty combo is also amazing.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

What to Do

  1. Toast your bread to your liking.
  2. Spread alot of jelly on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread more peanut butter on the other slice.
  4. Place the two slices together. Do not smush it.
  5. Enjoy
